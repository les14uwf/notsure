The Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) is a collection of telephone survey data. BRFSS contains basic demographic information along with acute and chronic disease status, healthcare and dental care access, insurance information, and a variety of socioeconomic indicators. The original dataset had >400,000 observations, which I filtered down to Florida only for this assessment. I believe this would be an excellent candidate for QR.
