Review

The Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) is a collection of telephone survey data. BRFSS contains deidentified demographic information along with acute and chronic disease status, healthcare and dental care access, insurance information, and a variety of socioeconomic indicators. The original dataset had >400,000 observations, which I filtered down to Florida residents and just a few variables for this initial assessment. I believe this would be an excellent candidate for QR.

brfssfl <- brfssfl %>%
  mutate(gen = case_when((GENHLTH == 1) ~ 'Excellent',
                         (GENHLTH == 2) ~ 'Very Good',
                         (GENHLTH == 3) ~ 'Good',
                         (GENHLTH == 4) ~ 'Fair',
                         (GENHLTH == 5) ~ 'Poor',
                         (GENHLTH == 7) ~ 'Not Sure',
                         (GENHLTH == 9) ~ 'Refused',))
brfssfl <- brfssfl %>%
  mutate(phy = case_when(
    PHYSHLTH >= 1 & PHYSHLTH <= 7 ~ '1-7',
    PHYSHLTH >= 8 & PHYSHLTH <= 14 ~ '8-14',
    PHYSHLTH >= 15 & PHYSHLTH <= 21 ~ '15-21',
    PHYSHLTH >= 22 & PHYSHLTH <= 30 ~ '>21',
    PHYSHLTH == 77 ~ 'Not Sure',
    PHYSHLTH == 88 ~ 'None',
    PHYSHLTH == 99 ~ 'Refused',
    TRUE ~ NA_character_
  ))
