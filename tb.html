Review

This dataset is from a randomized controlled trial (RCT) of an antibiotic treatment for tuberculosis. The study was published in 1948 in the British Medical Journal. It has 107 observations and 13 variables including dosages, gender, and baseline measures including patient condition, temperature, and cavitation. I do not believe this would be a good candidate for QR.
